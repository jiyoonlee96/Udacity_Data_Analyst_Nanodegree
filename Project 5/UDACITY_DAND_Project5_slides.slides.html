Effects of Flight Time and Day on Departure and Arrival Delay¶

by Ji Yoon Lee¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this report, I analyze flight features and their correlations with departure delay and arrival delay. The main features were time of day, time of week, origin state, city, airport, and carrier.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset analyzed is 2020 January US Domestic flightst. The dataset contains 22 features of 607346 flights (e.g., time, date, delay/cancellation status, origin, destination, air time, distance, carrier).

Distribution of Flight Departure Delay¶

13.63% of all flights departed more than 15 minutes later than the scheduled time. Departure delay takes on a large range of values from about -50 minutes to 2500 minutes (outliers too large had to be cut off). The distribution is skewed to the right with a mean around 5 minutes.

Distribution of Flight Arrival Delay¶

13.73% of all flights arrived more than 15 minutes later than the scheduled time. Arrival delay has a similar distribution and a similar range, but is shifted to the left with a mean around -10 minutes (arrived 10 minutes earlier than the scheduled time).

Distribution of Cancelled Flights¶

1.14%of all flights were cancelled.

Origin Airports, Cities, States, and Carriers with the most delayed Flights¶

  • The airports with the highest proportion of delayed flights are ABR, Aribama Incorporated, and ABR.
  • The cities with the highest proportion of delayed flights are Adak Island, AK and Aberdeen, SD.
  • The states with the highest proportion of delayed flights are Alaska, Florida, and Colorado.
  • The carriers with the highest proportion of delayed flights are Alaska Airlines, Allegiant Air, and Envoy Air.

Departure Delay and Arrival Delay by Day of Week and Time¶

  • Departure Delay : the average departure delay is the longest on Saturday afternoon, and shortest during Tuesday before noon.
  • Arrival Delay : the average arrival delay is the longest on Saturday afternoon, and shortest during Tuesday before noon (flights arrive on average 8 minutes before arrival time).
